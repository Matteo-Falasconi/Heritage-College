Cho-Han Rules

  1. Equipment: Two standard six-sided dice are used in the game.
  2. Objective: The objective of Cho-Han is to correctly guess whether the sum of the two dice will be an even number(cho) or an odd number (han).
  3. Betting: Players place their bets on either "cho" (even) or "han" (odd) before the dice are rolled.
  4. Rolling the dice: The dealer rolls the two dice, and the dice result is revealed to all players.
  5. Determining the outcome: If the sum of the two dice matches the player's bet (cho or han), they win. Otherwise, they lose.
  6. Payouts: If a player wins, they receive a payout typically equal to their original bet. If they lose, they forfeit their bet.
  7. Repeat: The game can be played in multiple rounds with new bets placed before each roll.
