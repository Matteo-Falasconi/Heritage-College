Focus On What is Important

In Falasconi’s Panini, we take pride in our freshly picked and ethically sourced ingredients. Originating in Italy, we are proud representatives of our countries number 1 Panini shop. Our Paninis are handcrafted to perfection to match everyone’s needs. We respect the individuality of everyone and make it our top priority to meet those requirements. You need it, we got it!

With our incredibly friendly family that runs this establishment, we can confidently say that no job will be left undone until the perfect condition is met. We offer a wide range of open hours so that you can feel anytime is a good time. Our friendly environment provides regular orders custom or preset with the addition of the option to request ingredients.
