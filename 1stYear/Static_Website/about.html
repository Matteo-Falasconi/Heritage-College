What Makes Us the Best

It all started in 2005, up on the Alps of Italy where a humble family of the Falasconi's owned a panini shop. The shop started from this small family owned business that was locally known for their top of the line panini's freshly made with all naturally harvested ingredients from their fields. The business started to gather the popularity all across Italy, and soon it was time for them to go international. We needed to expand through the internet, and this website was the child of that idea. We will bring the amazing taste of our paninis to all of you around the world. Not only that, but we have shop sites in 8 different countries and hope to expand even further. We take pride in our craft and will not use any non-local ingredients that aren't ethically sourced. Thank you for reading this if you have gotten this far. Hope to see you soon!
