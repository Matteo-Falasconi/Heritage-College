Meet the Family

Johnny Falasconi AKA Big Johnny is a well known family member. He is a personal favourite amongst the customers because of his morals and kind nature to help others. Whenever someone walks through that door he greets the customers with a warm welcome.

Francesco Falasconi is the main supervisor that deals with all the marketing of our shop. He is widely known as the DeVinci of paninis and can make anything normal look amazing. His amazing inovative talent is what keeps our company afloat.

Mario Falasconi is a man of little words but all those words pack a meaning. He never speaks until absolutely necessary and he keeps his work to himself. Though masterious he is a the master of all that is panini.

Beatrice Falasconi is the explorer of the group seeking out the best possible ingredients out there. From north to south whatever it is he will find a way to it and add it to his collection. He goes on journeys for weeks and months just to find the perfect taste. I wonder where he is now...
